Central PA Tourism Page (180 points)

Project Description:

Working with a design you create in Figma, you will build a single HTML page promoting the attractions and sites around Central Pennsylvania. You’re welcome to modify the topic—it might be favorite coffee shops or best trails to hike—be creative!

Your finished HTML page will feature 6-10 locations with accompanying images, brief descriptions, and links to external sites providing more information.

To drive your design decisions, you will perform some initial research. The final design should be published online via Github pages.

Process:

  1. Research
    • Come up with 5-10 questions regarding your topic and interview at least three (3) of your classmates and three (3) friends or family members.
    • With the responses, generate 3 fictional personas (consider using Figma's persona template, or create your own).
    • Research similar websites and create a list of 6-12 attractions based on your research.
  2. Design
    • Create a wireframe in Figma outlining the hierarchy of your page. No colors, photos, or fonts should be defined at this stage.
    • Photograph, illustrate, or find Creative Commons-licensed images to incorporate into your design.
    • Establish a color palette of at least 5 colors
    • Design a single desktop page in Figma and link on D2L.
  3. Development
    • Your page layout should incorporate at least one multi-column row.
    • Your design should use at least one (1) non-system web font.
    • Your page should include a favicon and a touch icon.
    • Your finished project should be published to Github pages and linked on D2L.
