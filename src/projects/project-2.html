Description:

For this project, you will choose a skill or topic that can be broken down into separate steps and explained over 5-10 web pages. Your topic could be following a cooking recipe, folding origami, or performing an oil change on a car.

Your finished project should contain an intuitive navigation and original illustrations or photography to help convey each step.

Process:

Research
  1. Find and critique three similar (3) websites that explain a skill. Submit your critiques on D2L.
Design
  1. Create a sitemap in FigJam outlining the pages of your site
  2. Create a wireframe in Figma of your interface
  3. Photograph, illustrate, or find Creative Commons-licensed images to incorporate into your design
  4. Establish a color palette of at least 5 colors
  5. Design your site in Figma and link on D2L
Development
  1. File names should be SEO-friendly
  2. Pages should use sticky navigation to indicate the current page
  3. Your finished project should be published to Github pages and linked on D2L
