Book Promotion Site (220 points)

Project Description:

For this assignment, you will be promoting Lewis Carroll's “Alice in Wonderland” and promoting it through a microsite with a maximum of five (5) pages. You can use this content: project-3-content.pdf or come up with your own material. As you are designing, bear in mind that while the text may no longer be protected by copyright, the artwork of the book cover may not be included.

Process:

  1. Research
    1. Find and critique three similar (3) websites that promote a book (you may have to visit author or publisher sites). Submit your critiques on D2L.
    2. Consider famous quotes to pull from the book, reviews to cite, and/or a bio on the author
  2. Design
    1. Create a sitemap in FigJam outlining the pages of your site
    2. Create a wireframe in Figma of your interface
    3. Site should be responsive--design for both desktop and mobile
    4. Consider creating a book cover design for your title
    5. Establish a color palette of at least 5 colors
    6. Design your site in Figma and link on D2L
  3. Development
    1. Your responsive design should not use a framework (Bootstrap, Tailwind, etc.)
    2. Your finished project should be published to Github pages and linked on D2L
